## 3. BUYER'S COMMITMENT

3.1 Buyer hereby confirms **its** genuine and serious intention to enter into a Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA) upon receipt of an acceptable Full Corporate Offer (FCO) from Seller.

3.2 Buyer confirms that it has the **financial capability** to open a Documentary Letter of Credit (DLC) in accordance with UCP 600, issued by a Top 50/100 World Bank by assets, within 7 (seven) banking days of SPA execution.

3.3 Buyer authorizes Seller to conduct standard Know-Your-Customer (KYC) verification on Buyer and **its** principals.

3.4 Upon receipt of FCO, Buyer undertakes to issue an **Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Order (ICPO)** within [3–5] banking days, if terms are acceptable.

---

## 4. SOFT PROBE AUTHORIZATION

Buyer hereby irrevocably authorizes Seller and/or **its** designated banking institution to conduct a non-intrusive **Soft Probe (bank-to-bank verification)** of Buyer's Proof of Funds (POF), strictly on a **no-debit basis**, without revealing account balances.

This authorization is limited to verification purposes only and does not constitute any debit instruction.

---

## 5. TRANSACTION PROCEDURE

Buyer acknowledges and accepts the following mandatory transaction sequence:

1. LOI (this document) → Seller
2. FCO → Buyer
3. POF (Proof of Funds) → Seller
4. ICPO → Seller
5. SPA → Executed by both parties
6. DLC → Issued by Buyer within 7 banking days
7. Shipment → As per SPA schedule

---

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Buyer agrees that all information received from Seller in connection with this transaction shall be treated as strictly **PRIVATE AND CONFIDENTIAL**. Buyer shall not disclose any such information to any third party without prior written consent of Seller.

Buyer and **its** representatives acknowledge being bound by the **NCNDA** (Non-Circumvention Non-Disclosure Agreement) executed in connection with this transaction.

---

## 7. VALIDITY

This Letter of Intent is valid for **3 (three) banking days** from the date of issue.

---

## 8. NO BINDING OBLIGATION

Save for the confidentiality provisions (Clause 6), this LOI does not create any binding obligation upon either party until execution of a formal SPA. However, Buyer represents that **its** intention expressed herein is genuine and made in good faith.
