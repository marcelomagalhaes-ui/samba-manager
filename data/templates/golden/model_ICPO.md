## 4. PROOF OF FUNDS (POF) — SOFT PROBE AUTHORIZATION

4.1 Buyer hereby **irrevocably authorizes** Seller and/or **its** designated banking correspondent to conduct a **non-intrusive, non-debit Soft Probe** of Buyer's Proof of Funds, strictly limited to confirming the existence of funds sufficient to cover the total contract value.

4.2 This authorization expressly **prohibits any debit, lien, or encumbrance** on Buyer's account as a result of said probe.

4.3 Buyer represents that funds are **unencumbered, lawfully sourced**, and immediately available for the purposes of this transaction.

4.4 POF shall be provided within **[3] banking days** of Seller's request.

---

## 5. PERFORMANCE BOND

5.1 Buyer accepts the requirement to provide a **Performance Bond of 2% (two percent)** of the total contract value, to be wired via Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) to Seller's designated account within **[5] banking days** of SPA execution.

5.2 The Performance Bond is fully refundable upon successful completion of all shipments and settlement of all payment obligations.

5.3 In the event of Buyer's failure to fulfill **its** obligations, the Performance Bond shall be forfeited to Seller as liquidated damages.

---

## 6. TRANSACTION PROCEDURE ACCEPTANCE

Buyer acknowledges and accepts the following mandatory sequence:

1. LOI → FCO → POF/ICPO → SPA → Draft DLC → Proforma Invoice → Operative DLC → Shipment

Buyer shall not request deviations from this sequence without prior written consent of Seller.

## 7. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Buyer represents and warrants that:

7.1 **Its** execution of this ICPO has been duly authorized by all necessary corporate action, evidenced by the Board Resolution or Power of Attorney attached hereto as **Annex A**.

7.2 **Its** signatory has full legal authority to bind the Buyer corporation.

7.3 The funds to be used for this transaction are of **lawful origin** and comply with all applicable anti-money laundering (AML) and Know-Your-Customer (KYC) regulations.

7.4 Buyer is not subject to any international sanctions, trade embargoes, or regulatory restrictions that would prevent **its** participation in this transaction.
