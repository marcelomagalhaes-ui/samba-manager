## ARTICLE 4 — IRREVOCABILITY AND PAYMENT OBLIGATION

4.1 Seller and Buyer jointly and irrevocably guarantee that the fees specified in Article 2 shall be:
(a) Paid **simultaneously** with each principal payment to Seller;
(b) Wired directly from Seller's bank to each Intermediary's bank account upon receipt of funds;
(c) **Not subject to deduction, offset, or delay** for any reason;
(d) Payable for **the entire duration** of the contract, including all renewals and extensions thereof.

4.2 This obligation is **irrevocable** and shall survive any dispute between Buyer and Seller.

4.3 Seller's banking institution shall be instructed, via a signed letter of authorization (copy to each Intermediary), to process the fee transfers automatically upon receipt of each DLC payment.

---

## ARTICLE 5 — PROTECTION AGAINST CIRCUMVENTION

5.1 Seller and Buyer irrevocably agree not to bypass, exclude, circumvent, or replace any Intermediary from this transaction or any subsequent transaction arising from introductions made under this Agreement, without **its** prior written consent.

5.2 Any attempt to circumvent any Intermediary shall entitle the aggrieved Intermediary to:
(a) Claim the full fee as if the transaction had been completed;
(b) Seek injunctive relief and other available remedies.

---

## ARTICLE 6 — TERM AND RENEWAL

6.1 This IMFPA shall be effective from the date of execution and shall remain in force for the **full duration of the SPA**, including all renewals, extensions, and add-on volumes.

6.2 Any renewal or extension of the underlying SPA shall automatically extend the protections of this IMFPA without further formality.
