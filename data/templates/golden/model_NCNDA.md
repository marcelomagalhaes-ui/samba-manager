## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to explore certain business opportunities and may disclose to each other certain confidential and proprietary information;

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to protect **their** respective interests and the integrity of **their** business relationships;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1 — DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Confidential Information"** means any and all information disclosed by one Party to another, whether in written, oral, electronic, or any other form, including but not limited to:
- Names, addresses, and contact details of suppliers, buyers, brokers, or intermediaries
- Pricing, volumes, margins, routes, and commercial conditions
- Transaction structures, contract terms, and negotiation strategies
- Business relationships, contacts, and client lists
- Source and destination of products, logistics routes

1.2 **"Circumvention"** means any direct or indirect attempt to:
- Contact or transact with any contact introduced by another Party without **its** knowledge and consent
- Bypass, remove, or replace any Party from the transaction chain or business relationship
- Obtain information or complete transactions using contacts, leads, or opportunities introduced by another Party without **its** involvement or compensation

---

## ARTICLE 2 — NON-CIRCUMVENTION

2.1 Each Party irrevocably agrees that it shall not, directly or indirectly, circumvent, avoid, bypass, or obviate any other Party's interest in any transaction or business relationship arising from or connected to information or contacts disclosed under this Agreement.

2.2 No Party shall contact, negotiate with, or enter into any agreement with any contact, supplier, buyer, broker, or intermediary introduced by another Party without:
(a) The prior written consent of the introducing Party; and
(b) Ensuring that the introducing Party receives **its** agreed compensation.

2.3 The non-circumvention obligations of this Article shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement for a period of **2 (two) years**.

---

## ARTICLE 3 — NON-DISCLOSURE

3.1 Each Party agrees to:
(a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;
(b) Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing Party;
(c) Use Confidential Information solely for the purposes of evaluating and conducting the transaction(s) contemplated under this Agreement;
(d) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses to protect **its** own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

3.2 Exceptions: Confidential Information does not include information that:
(a) Was in the receiving Party's possession before disclosure;
(b) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party;
(c) Is required to be disclosed by applicable law or court order (with prompt prior notice to the disclosing Party).

---

## ARTICLE 4 — PENALTY FOR BREACH

4.1 Any Party that breaches the non-circumvention or non-disclosure provisions of this Agreement shall be liable to pay the aggrieved Party(ies):
- **Liquidated damages:** USD [Amount] (or 5% of the gross value of any circumvented transaction, whichever is greater)
- Plus all legal costs and expenses incurred in enforcing this Agreement

4.2 The aggrieved Party shall also be entitled to seek injunctive relief and any other available remedies at law or in equity.

4.3 The Parties agree that the above damages are a genuine pre-estimate of loss and are not a penalty.

---

## ARTICLE 5 — TERM

5.1 This Agreement shall be effective as of the date first written above and shall remain in force for **2 (two) years**, unless extended by mutual written agreement.

5.2 The non-circumvention and confidentiality obligations shall **survive termination** of this Agreement for an additional period of **2 (two) years** following expiration or termination.

## ARTICLE 7 — GENERAL PROVISIONS

7.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties concerning **its** subject matter.

7.2 **Amendments.** This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by all Parties.
