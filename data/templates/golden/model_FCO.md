## SECTION 4 — TRANSACTION PROCEDURE

The transaction shall proceed in the following mandatory sequence:

1. **LOI** (Letter of Intent) from Buyer → Seller acknowledges receipt
2. **FCO** (Full Corporate Offer) from Seller → Buyer reviews
3. **POF** (Proof of Funds) from Buyer → verified by Seller's financial team
4. **ICPO** (Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Order) from Buyer → Seller confirms
5. **SPA** (Sale and Purchase Agreement) → signed by both parties
6. **Draft DLC** → Buyer submits for Seller review within 3 banking days of SPA
7. **Proforma Invoice** → Seller issues upon DLC approval
8. **Operative DLC** → Buyer issues within 7 banking days of SPA execution
9. **Shipment** → Seller arranges within [X] days of operative DLC receipt

## SECTION 5 — OTHER CONSIDERATIONS

5.1 **Performance Bond:** Upon execution of SPA, Buyer shall wire 2% (two percent) of the total contract value as Performance Bond via T/T to Seller's account within [X] banking days.

5.2 **Non-Performance:** In the event Seller fails to deliver, Seller shall refund the Performance Bond and pay Buyer a penalty equivalent to 0.5% of the undelivered shipment value per day of delay, up to a maximum of 5%.

5.3 **Force Majeure:** Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform **its** obligations if such failure results from circumstances beyond **its** reasonable control, including but not limited to: acts of God, war, government embargo, port strikes, or natural disasters. The affected party shall notify the other within 5 business days.

5.4 **Entire Agreement:** This FCO supersedes all previous communications and constitutes the entire offer between the parties with respect to **its** subject matter.

## SECTION 6 — INSPECTION

6.1 Quantity and quality shall be determined at the **port of loading** by an internationally recognized and neutral inspection entity jointly selected from:
- SGS (Société Générale de Surveillance)
- CCIC (China Certification & Inspection Group)
- Bureau Veritas
- Intertek

6.2 Inspection costs shall be shared **equally (50%/50%)** between Buyer and Seller.

6.3 The inspector's findings shall be final and binding on both parties regarding:
- Certificate of Quality
- Certificate of Quantity / Weight
- Phytosanitary Certificate
- Certificate of Origin (Form A or CO as applicable)

6.4 Weight tolerance: **±5%** on each shipment.
